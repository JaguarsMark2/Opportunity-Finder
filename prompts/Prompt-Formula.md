Prompt Formula:
​I have a business idea, and I want to discuss it with you to decide whether it's feasible or not. I'll send the idea to you, and then I want you to analyze it, ask questions to gather more context, and help me determine whether my idea is good or not. I do not want you to be a 'yes man'; if something is a bad idea, I need you to tell me so. Here's my idea: [Insert Business Idea] What other factors might I need to consider? What do you think of the feasibility of the plan so far?

Quick Description:
​This prompt is perfect for aspiring entrepreneurs who want to avoid common pitfalls and ensure their business ideas are solid before investing time and resources. Use it to refine your concept, enhance your strategy, and increase your chances of success.

How to Use:

Copy the prompt formula above and paste it into ChatGPT.
Replace [Insert Business Idea] with your business idea. Go into as much or as little detail as you want; this prompt is designed to fill in whatever gaps there are in your business idea.
Send the prompt! ChatGPT will break down your idea into key components such as market demand, competition, operational challenges, and legal considerations and offer a detailed critique.
Discuss the feasibility of the idea with ChatGPT. Expect ChatGPT to ask clarifying questions, prompting you to think more deeply about aspects of your idea you might not have fully considered. This ensures the feedback is tailored to your specific context.
